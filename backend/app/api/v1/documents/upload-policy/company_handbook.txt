SECTION 1: CORE ATTENDANCE & WORK HOURS POLICY
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1.1 Operational Framework
The standard workweek consists of 40 operational hours, typically structured as 8 hours per day, Monday through Friday. Core collaboration hours are defined between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM local time.

1.2 Attendance Tracking & Clock-In Protocols
All personnel are required to log daily attendance via the software portal. 
- "Clock-In" events must be registered prior to 10:00 AM daily.
- "Clock-Out" events must be registered upon completion of the daily shift.
- Multi-click tracking telemetry is safely intercepted by the system; secondary clock-ins on the same calendar day will preserve the original entry.
- Failure to log attendance for three consecutive working days without a submitted leave request triggers an automated system notification to Human Resources.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2: LEAVE ALLOCATION & COMPLIANCE GUARDRAILS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.1 Routine Leave Framework
Full-time personnel receive a baseline allocation of 12 Sick Leaves and 15 Casual Leaves per fiscal cycle. 

2.2 Burnout & Anomaly Prevention Guardrails
The workforce analytics core dynamically scans leave request patterns to safeguard employee health and corporate stability. The following parameters represent anomalous or non-compliant behaviors:
- Concentrated Pattern Flagging: Requesting more than 3 consecutive days of leave alongside adjacent weekends without a formal medical certificate.
- Sick Leave Volatility: Consuming more than 4 allocated sick days within a single 30-day operational sprint.
- Concurrency Anomaly: Simultaneous high-duration leave requests across more than 40% of a critical engineering or operations department.

SECTION 3: COMPENSATION, REWARDS, AND FINANCES
------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.1 Payroll Disbursal Lifecycle
